Hello! I am a 19 year-old systems researcher mainly focused on kernel internals, hypervisors, CPUs and security module architectures.

This is a one-file portfolio page to highlight some of my work and journey! Feel free to explore and reach out if you'd like to connect!

Research Journey
2015 - The Beginning of Everything.

Was always into computers since childhood. Assembled my first computer from components at the age of 9.

2015-2019 - Figuring things out.

Games were fun, but was more interested in messing around with Operating System installations and drive paritions; Moved around Windows 7, 8 and XP for fun every week.

Fixed up neighbourhood computers and father's office computers when they broke.

2020 - The Rabbit Hole.

Lockdown started because of the pandemic, had nothing to do and started exploring low-level languages like C/C++/Asm and self taught myself how virtual memory worked.

2021-2023 - Windows Kernel Development

Deep dive into Windows kernel internals. Started messing around with the with kernel and had the motivation to exploit it.

Built own tooling and researched components of renowned anti-cheats, anti-malware, EDRs, XDRs and other security products.

2024-2025 (Present) - Hypervisor & CPU Research and finding a new interest.

Developed interest in hypervisors and emulation with precise control in general, spent most of my time reading up articles and information about CPU and Security module architecture; P.S.

Found a new interest in music.
